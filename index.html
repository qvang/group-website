ABOUT US

Not everyone can afford to attend an intensive coding school or obtain a computer science education, and neither of those options is suitable for everyone. The goal of this initiative is to close the gap for those who want a top-notch education but are attempting to achieve it on their own.

Personalised Learning

Students work at their own speed, filling in knowledge gaps before advancing their study.

Trusted Content

Developed by professionals, Codex's collection of reliable computer science projects and tutorials is always free for students and teachers.

Tools to empower teachers

Teachers can use Codex to pinpoint knowledge gaps, customise lessons, and address each student's requirements.
